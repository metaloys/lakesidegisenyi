Our Story

A Taste of Lakeside Living

Nestled along the shores of the breathtaking Lake Kivu in Gisenyi, Lakeside Bar Beach & Restaurant has been the heartbeat of the local dining scene. Our kitchen draws from the freshest local catch and seasonal produce, crafting dishes that honour Rwandan heritage with a contemporary touch.

From sunrise breakfasts watching the mist lift off the water, to candlelit dinners as the lake reflects a thousand stars — every moment here is curated for those who appreciate the finer things in life.
